GreenWall

INTEGRATING INFRASTRUCTURE, DESIGN, AND BOTANY

The GreenWall project is an on campus outdoor green wall aiming to bring a year round living space to Tech. The project focuses on integrating hydroponic techniques and carpentry with the science of botany using design thinking. We are currently in the design phase with members working on developing CAD models and physical mini mockups. In the following months the project members will be collaborating to further develop and integrate the water system of our wall, researching native plants to have on our wall, and constructing the frame of the wall

If you are interested in joining our project, please reach out to one or both of our project managers for more information!
