Engineers for a Sustainable World

ESW at Northwestern University (ESWNU) empowers students to take on leadership roles in applying the principles of sustainability to their present and future work through action-based engineering projects and the education of ourselves and our community on sustainability.

What We Do

  • Design

    Help design, build, and implement long lasting sustainability solutions in our community.

  • Educate

    Gain both the technical and professional skills needed to tackle the world's biggest problems.

  • Unite

    Collaborate with students from all backgrounds to forge solutions to sustainability challenges.
